    What Sets Us Apart

    Charlize College offers a wide array of over 20 diploma and certificate programs across four campuses in the Kitchener-Waterloo Area. Our curriculum is crafted to expand your knowledge, enhance your analytical abilities, and equip you for success in a professional setting.
  • Exceptional Graduation and Employment Rates: We maintain strong ties with industry partners, ensuring that our graduates enjoy the highest employment rates in Ontario. Our alumni are highly prepared and in demand in the workforce.
  • Lifetime Refresher Courses: Upon graduation, you can take advantage of ongoing education through our Lifetime Refresher Course, allowing you to update your skills whenever necessary.
  • Industry-Experienced Instructors: Our faculty is composed of seasoned professionals committed to delivering top-notch education and fostering an optimal learning atmosphere.
  • World-Class Education: Our campuses are outfitted with cutting-edge technology. We provide small class sizes and an interactive teaching style that fosters a productive learning environment.
  • Diverse Student Population: With students hailing from over 30 countries, Charlize College is a thriving multicultural center. This diversity creates a rich and inclusive educational experience.
  • Internships: Numerous diploma programs at Charlize College include internships organized by the college, ensuring that you gain practical experience and confidence in your field before graduating.
  • Flexible Scheduling: We offer flexible day, evening, and weekend classes to accommodate your busy schedule, enabling you to pursue your education without compromising your other commitments.
