OUR PROGRAMS

If you aim to become a respected professional, obtaining the proper training is essential! Charlize College offers Diploma and Certificate programs designed to make you job-ready. Our programs are rich in information, providing you with quality, career-focused vocational training without the need for years in post-secondary education. Many of our Diploma programs include internships, allowing you to graduate with confidence in your skills and abilities. Charlize College takes full responsibility for securing a placement for you in a suitable organization. To accommodate your busy schedule, we offer flexible day, evening, and weekend classes.

IT Programs


The IT industry is experiencing unprecedented growth, presenting a wealth of opportunities for aspiring professionals. Whether you're looking to break into the field or advance your current career, our comprehensive training programs provide a distinct advantage. With our expert-led courses, you'll gain the skills and knowledge needed to excel in various IT roles. Equip yourself with cutting-edge expertise and stand out in the competitive IT landscape with our specialized career training.

Medical Programs


here's no question that the medical field is experiencing significant growth. Law Programs


Our law program at Charlize College is renowned for its excellence, offering comprehensive training, distinguished faculty, and extensive practical experience. Our curriculum is designed to provide a deep understanding of legal principles, combined with real-world application through internships and moot court sessions. Students benefit from the mentorship of experienced legal professionals and have access to cutting-edge resources and research opportunities.

Business Programs

Whether you aspire to manage multinational corporations or launch your own startup, our programs offer the perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
